Welcome to my homepage

Education

I got my B.S. degree at East China University of Science and Techenology (aka, ECUST) in 2018. Then I moved to Peking University as a Ph.D graduate (2018-2024, supposed to gradute in 2024). I am working on the rapid growth of seed black holes (BHs) at early Universe, and their coevolution with host galaxies, under the supervision and guidance of Kohei Inayoshi. I am also interested in the interplay between supermassive BHs (SMBHs) and intracluster medium (AGN feedback).
