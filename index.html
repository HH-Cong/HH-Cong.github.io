About Me (Haojie Hu; 胡 豪杰)

Hi there.

This is Haojie Hu from Peking University. "Haojie" is my first name, it is written as "豪杰" in Chinese character, which means "a hero, a brave man". The pronunciation is like "How Jet(with "t" silient)". My family name is "Hu", with pronunciation like "who". In China, we have family name comes first, so it is wirtten as "胡豪杰". You can just call me "Haojie [How Jet(with "t" silient)]" or just "Jie [Jet(with "t" silient)]".

I am currently a graduate student in Department of Astronomy, School of physics, majoring in astrophysics. My research interests focus on SuperMassive Black Holes (SMBHs). I am interested in the formation and evolution of SMBHs at high redshifts (z>6), and their co-evolution with host galaxies (the co-evolution scenario). The interplay between central SMBHs and their host galaxies especially in Active Galactic Nuclei (aka, AGN feeding and feedback ), always facinates me. I try to understand the growth pathway of seed BH to SMBHs at high redshifts via numerical simulations. The ongoing James Webb Space Telescope (JWST) is gradually revealing the secrets of Universe at high redshift. Meanwhile, numerical simulations provide unique approaches to help us pridict and understand the mysterious SMBHs at high redshift universe. You can find out more details in Resaerch panel.

My Ph.D advisor is Kohei Inayoshi (From KIAA, PKU). I also collaborate with many excellent people around the world. They are Prof. Zoltan Haiman from Columbia University, Dr. Yu Qiu from PKU, Prof. Rolf Kuiper from Universität Duisburg-Essen, Wenxiu Li from PKU, Prof. Eliot Quataert from Princeton University, Dr. Marie-Lou Gendron-Marsolais from Instituto de Astrofísica de Andalucía (IAA-CSIC), Prof. Tamara Bogdanović from Georgia Institute of Technology, Dr.Julie Hlavacek-Larrondo from Université de Montréal, Prof.Luis C. Ho from PKU, and Prof.Brian R. McNamara from University of Waterloo, and so on. Please find out more in Collaborators panel.

Besides astronomy and astrophysics, I also enjoy travelling and photographing. If you are interested in my work or my interests, you are very welcomed to discuss with me. My email is hhj_pku@pku.edu.cn. Feel free to send emails to me.

Thanks for getting here. Wish you a very happy morning, a happy afternoon, a happy evening, and a happy day.

Yours,
Haojie Hu
